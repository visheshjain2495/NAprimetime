PRIME TIME

Nation Arts Prime Time is a unique arthouse cinema chain nestled within the vibrant atmosphere of a classic Cuban restaurant on Grand Concourse in the Bronx, New York.

Our mission is passionately dedicated to uplifting and showcasing the extraordinary works of first-time independent filmmakers and creators, illuminating their artistry for both local communities and global audiences.

We strive to tackle the distribution hurdles that many filmmakers face, particularly those addressing pressing social, political, and culturally significant themes in their films.

ZONA DE CUBA

Guests are warmly invited to indulge in the rich, multicultural offerings of Zona de Cuba's bar while enjoying captivating movie nights, complete with live music and exceptional entertainment.

We proudly offer an array of services tailored to diverse needs, including private screenings, hourly rentals, one-time showings, and two-week licensing agreements. Our pricing structure is thoughtfully designed to correspond with innovative distribution strategies and screening plans, ensuring that every filmmaker has the opportunity to shine.
